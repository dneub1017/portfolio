ABOUT ME

Background:

I am a Baton Rouge native. I attended Broadmoor HS and later LSU. I have been involved in athletics almost my entire life. I began playing football in 5th grade and have played or coached every season since. My favorite subject in school was always math. I have also always been into computers and technology. I wanted to learn to code so I took a class through codehs.com, which got also me started teaching computer science. I am now currently taking a web development course through Udemy.com to further my education in coding. Web Design has become my favorite aspect of coding. When I was in high school I would help out at my family's printing business doing typsetting (business cards, letterheads, flyers, etc. ) and Web Design is the modern technical version of that.

Professional:

I got my first teaching job right out of school at False River Academy in New Roads. After a year I left for St. Michael High School in Baton Rouge which was a much shorter commute. At St. Michael I taught Algebra 1 and/or Geometry. I was at St. Michael for 9 years. During that time I was also on the search committe when we decided to go 1:1 with technology for the student body. This was a great learning experience and it also introduced me to a new way of doing things in my classroom. At St. Michael I also coached football and track & field.

After 9 years at St. Michael I left for University Lab School. This was a great opportunity for me and my family. At U-High I began by teaching just Algebra 1 and/or Geometry. As I started to learn some basic coding skills I wanted to teach computer science so I begged for the opportunity. I am currently in my third year teaching computer science. After the first year the course changed to AP Computer Science Principles. This course is meant to be somewhat of a introduction into the subject so students learn a variety things. The first semester is spent mostly coding HTML, CSS, and Javacript. The second semester is more how things work.

We have two biological children and currently have 1 foster child. We live on family land in St. Gabriel. Family is very important to me, which is part of the reason for the possible career change. I have been teaching/coaching for 16 years and the idea of getting into the tech field is very exciting to me.
